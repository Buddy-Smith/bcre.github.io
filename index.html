Battle Creek Research and Enterprise?

What is Battle Creek Research and Enterprise?

Battle Creek Research and Enterprise (BC R&E) is a science, technology and research based company. Although we are still in the start up phases, Daniel Roy and Brice Partee are seeking to bring together scientists, engineers, designers, programers and like minded people together to team up and develop sustainable technologies and practices. Forming a company will allow us to share our skills and more easily acquire funding for fun projects. Our main goal through BC R&E is to innovate and make the world a better place through our research and development, making a profit and earning some credibility is just an added bonus!

So what's been done?

We’ve been actively working to establish a professional setting and foundation for the company. Many of the things we’ve been working on will be important to make the business run smoothly. This includes establishing a company Email and working to establish a firm online presence. This helps us reach out and make more connections, receive funding, establish company contracts and keep employees up to date and in touch with the latest company news.Our professional Business Plan is also in the works and near completion, this too will help us have a clean and professional presentation of our company when we are seeking funding from potential investors.

Exciting potential contract with A2Z Security.

Exciting potential contract with A2Z Security! A2Z is a company working to expand their range of electronic products. We reached out in an email regarding how we could do business with A2Z. They were quick to respond with plenty of opportunities. They seem to be a company positioned to market and sell electronics but they also mentioned they could help us with the manufacturing process. They provided a clear list of developments and technologies they would like us to work on and even said they have funding prepared for some of the projects in mind. We’ll have more on this for anyone that will be with us in the next coming weeks.
